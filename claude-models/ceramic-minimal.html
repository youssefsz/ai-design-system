Principles

01

Intentional space

Whitespace is the primary design material. It creates hierarchy, establishes rhythm, and gives content room to communicate without competition.

02

Material honesty

Surfaces are what they appear to be. No simulated textures, no artificial depth. Light and shadow are used truthfully, not decoratively.

03

Typographic calm

Two typefaces, used with discipline. Serif for identity and emphasis. Sans-serif for explanation and navigation. No mixing roles.

04

Functional beauty

An object is beautiful when it works well. Ornamentation that does not serve function is removed during refinement.
